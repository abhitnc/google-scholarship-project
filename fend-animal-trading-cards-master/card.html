Leopard


The leopard, also known as pard, is one of the five species in the genus Panthera, a member of the Felidae.

  • Scientific name:Panthera pardus
  • Speed: 58 km/h (Running)
  • Lifespan:12 – 17 years
  • Trophic level:Carnivorous

The scientific name of the leopard is Panthera pardus. The generic name Panthera derives from Latin via Greek πάνθηρ (pánthēr).The term "panther", whose first recorded use dates back to the 13th century AD, generally refers to the leopard, and less often to the cougar and the jaguar.Alternative origins suggested for Panthera include an Indo-Iranian word meaning "white-yellow" or "pale".
